I love scoring movies and games. I've mostly worked with electronically produced or self-recorded scores because it's really fun, cheap, and convenient. I've also enjoyed writing for ensembled. All of my work so far has been in a DIY environment. This page has a few examples of music I've made to picture - I've also made lots of other music, which you can check out on my music page.

This is a spec score for the game Elengard:Ascension that I made for the Indie Game Music Contest. It's a first draft of an idea. The only recorded instrument is clarinet, the rest are VSTs.

June Wyoming is an indie short made for a 48hr film festival in 2023. My score for it includes a theme song, a cover of a pop song, and some more tension-building sounds.

Inventing Denmark is a comedy sketch made for YouTube group Town Talk. The story is very bizarre and the score tries its best to enrich that.

Legals is a stop motion animation I made & scored just for fun with my friend Ledah Finck. The score is mostly electronics and processed clarinet, but also includes an original song.
