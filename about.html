Welcome to my website! I'm a Brooklyn-based artist who focuses mostly on music production and performance art. I'm involved with the rock band You Want Milk, performance group Rhymes With Orange, podcast Hello If Anything, and I also have a Bandcamp with some solo singer-songwriter stuff. Outside of those I play jazz piano, do some scoring work for movies and games, some standup, some video art, and generally try to get myself into miscellaneous scrapes. I love organizing and hosting events, especially JetLAG Festival. I also have a masters' in math, LOL. The best ways to get in touch with me are via my Instagram @god_yes or just send me an email: .
