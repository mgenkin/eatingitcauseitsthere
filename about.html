Hi! I'm a Brooklyn-based musician and theater artist. Thanks for visiting my website! I like making stuff that is fun and surprising, I'm interested in clown and devised theater. As a musician I play jazz piano and songwriter guitar and produce electronic tracks for theater and film. I have some solo acoustic music out as well. I love DIY and low-budget art and I put on shows in my basement in Bushwick. In my day jobs I work with kids as a substitute teacher, birthday clown, music facilitator, math team coach, etc. I have a masters in math and a data science background that I no longer associate with.
