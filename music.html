I'm a multi-instrumentalist and make music for lots of different reasons, usually either to express myself, to connect with someone, or to create a vibe for some other art. Keyboard is my main instrument, which is weirdly not reflected in what's linked here. I also do songwriting, composing, and electronic production.

Honestly Garbage is a lo-fi indie album I made in 2024. It's simple and vulnerable, bordering on silly.

Hello If Anything is a mumblecore podcast that I co-host and co-edit with my collaborator Devuroasts. I produce the soundtrack for it too.

You Want Milk is a rock band I've been in since 2021. I co-write the music and play guitar, bass, keyboard, and vocals, depending on the arrangement. For the latest album I was mostly playing guitar and singing.

Nettle is a piece I co-wrote and play clarinet on. I also helped make the video. This piece is one of the first I've done with standard music notation.
