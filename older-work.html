This is older work that I'm no longer actively developing. For current work, check out my homepage.

I've been in lots of bands over the years. In reverse chronological order, there was He Who Understands Baboon, Tapestry Girl, Hausdorff ,... I'll spare you the high school & middle school bands.

I make these tracks annually for different events and to reflect on my life. I intend to continue, but I skipped 2023 so I'm considering it inactive for now.

For about 3 years I and a rotating cast of friends ran a basement venue in Bushwick called The Terrarium . A lot of beautiful things happened there and I really miss it. I hope I get to do something like it soon.

I also started an open mic at beloved Bushwick coffeehouse Caffeine Underground. I'm no longer involved, but my friend Noah is still running it!
